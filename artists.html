Jane Doe, Sculpting

Jane Doe is an American sculptor, of mixed African-American and Native American heritage. She was the first African-American and Native American sculptor to achieve national and then international prominence. Her preferred method to use is Clay Firing. However, Jane is well knowledged in all forms of sculpting.

Anna Ross, Painting

Anna Ross is a Canadian artist based in New York City. Wise works in sculpture, drawing, video art, and oil painting. Ross is known for her stylized and humorous approach to both still life and figurative painting. Her favorite style is abstract and it is one of her most popular classes.

Jon Pun, Photographer

Jon Pun is an American photographer and educator known for his large-scale art photography and street photography portraits, including American adolescents in relation to their community, and other often marginalized subjects. He also an abstract painter and will often help out on our painting classes.
